Pokémon Fun Facts

Did you know Venusaur's flower is said to take on vivid colors if it gets plenty of nutrition and sunlight? The flower's aroma soothes the emotions of people.

Habitat Information

Venusaur are commonly found in grassy plains and forests where they can absorb the sun's rays.

Abilities

Venusaur's ability "Overgrow" powers up Grass-type moves when the Pokémon's HP is low. It can also have the hidden ability "Chlorophyll," which boosts its Speed in sunlight.

Lore & Background

Venusaur, the Seed Pokémon, and the final form of Bulbasaur. The plant blooms when it is absorbing solar energy. It stays on the move to seek sunlight.

Evolution Methods

Bulbasaur evolves into Ivysaur starting at level 16 and then into Venusaur starting at level 32. In certain games, Venusaur can further evolve into Mega Venusaur using a Venusaurite.

Competitive Battling Tips

Venusaur is a versatile contender in competitive battles, thanks to its solid dual typing and robust stats. Utilizing Venusaur's Chlorophyll ability alongside a Sunny Day teammate can turn it into a formidable sweeper. Its wide movepool, including Sleep Powder and Sludge Bomb, allows it to fulfill various roles effectively.
